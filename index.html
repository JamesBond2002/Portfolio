Color Switch 2.0

Made the replica of the famous game Color Switch. The Technologies used were JavaFx and CSS. The objective of the game is to collect as many stars as possible by manoeuvring the obstacles. If you hit any obstacle, you can continue with your earned stars or the game ends if you have none and then you have to start from the beginning. This game is endless like the classic mode of original game.

Who will park the Car?

Made a multiplayer game using Arduino and MIT app inventor. The game consists of 2 mobile controlled car which will be controlled by 2 different mobile phones using bluetooth. The objective of the game is to park the car at a given position the one who parks the car first wins the game. I also used Ultrasonic sensor to prevent the car from accident.

Data Visualization

A website made to visualize the data of Cricket matches won by a respective country in a specific type of match (Test, ODI, T20) and the total matches played by the country in that type of match.
